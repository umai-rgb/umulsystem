studio kembara

Studio Kembara Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian film production company established in 2013 by Abdul Rahman Mat Dali and Shahruddin Mat Dali. The company has gained recognition for producing impactful films, most notably *Mat Kilau: Kebangkitan Pahlawan*, which became one of Malaysia's highest-grossing films. The movie highlights the historical legacy of the Malay warrior Mat Kilau and promotes patriotism, culture, and religious values. Beyond filmmaking, Studio Kembara has ventured into digital entertainment, such as creating culturally-themed video games and exploring virtual reality technologies. Their collaboration with other innovative companies aims to educate and engage younger audiences while preserving Malay heritage and traditions.
